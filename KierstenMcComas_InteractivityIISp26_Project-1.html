getting into the gym

Throughout middle and high school, I didn't really consider myself a particularly athletic person. I took up several sports as a kid but they were never really my thing. It wasn't until my senior year of high school that I would be encouraged to work out officially. I'd watch transformation videos of other people and knew that it would never be me. After many excuses and protests however, I gave in. It took a few weeks for me to actually start enjoying the work I was doing, but once it took hold I realized just how beneficial it started to be in my life.
